Gravitational Lensing

Click and drag the white blob on the left panel? See the crazy things on the right panel? A massive object bends the path of light and forms distorted images, kinda what lens in your glass does.

The four bright spots that you first saw, is called an Einstein cross. It is produced when the lensing massive object looks elliptical in shape.

Gravitational waves

Click on the following panels to "hear" the bombastic sound of colliding black holes. The sound starts when the black holes are orbiting and it peaks when they hit each other.
